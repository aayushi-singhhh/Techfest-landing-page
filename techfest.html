About TechFest

TechFest is an annual technology festival that brings together students, professionals, and industry leaders from around the world. Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and technological advancement.

This year's theme is "Sustainable Technology for a Better Tomorrow", focusing on how technology can help solve some of the world's most pressing environmental and social challenges.

As a campus ambassador, you'll play a crucial role in spreading awareness about the event, organizing pre-events on your campus, and representing your institution at the national level.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the responsibilities of a campus ambassador?

As a campus ambassador, you'll be responsible for promoting TechFest on your campus, organizing pre-events, coordinating with student groups, and acting as the main point of contact between your institution and the TechFest team.

How much time do I need to commit?

The time commitment is flexible, typically around 5-10 hours per week during the promotion period. Most tasks can be scheduled around your academic commitments.

What are the benefits of being an ambassador?

Benefits include certificates of recognition, letters of recommendation, exclusive merchandise, networking opportunities with industry professionals, and potential internship opportunities.

Can students from any college apply?

Yes, we welcome applications from students of all accredited colleges and universities. We value diversity and are looking for passionate individuals regardless of their institution.
